Essential Forms for Family Caregivers and Care Teams

Important: These forms allow healthcare providers to share protected health information with designated family members and care team members. Complete separate forms for each healthcare provider (doctor, home health agency, hospital, etc.). Keep copies for your records and provide originals to each provider.

  • Form 1: General HIPAA Authorization for family members
  • Form 2: Care Team Authorization for home care providers
  • Form 3: Emergency Contact Authorization

Legal Note: These forms are templates. Consult with an elder law attorney for complex situations or if you need forms for specific state requirements.

üìù Important Reminders

  • Keep copies: Make copies of all signed forms for your family records
  • Distribute to providers: Give original forms to each healthcare provider and care agency
  • Update regularly: Review and update forms annually or when contact information changes
  • Carry with you: Keep a copy of emergency authorization in wallet or purse
  • Legal review: Consider having an elder law attorney review forms for complex family situations
  • Revocation rights: Remember you can revoke these authorizations at any time in writing
State-Specific Requirements: Some states may have additional requirements for HIPAA authorizations. Check with your healthcare providers about any state-specific forms that may be needed.
